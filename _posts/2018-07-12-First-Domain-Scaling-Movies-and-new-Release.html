--- layout: layout title: First Domain Scaling Movies and new Release of InterPile ---

The first videos are online showing configurations corresponding to the sandpile identity dynamics induced by a given harmonic fields at the same or scaled times for increasing domain sizes. This visualizes both the capability of our approach for renormalization, as well as the super-position of different time scales.

We also released a new beta of InterPile (beta 3), with which these videos can be easily generated. Feel free to send us your best videos!
