Harmonic Sandpile Dynamics

For an explanation of the harmonic sandpile dynamics, we refer to our manuscript:

Moritz Lang and Mikhail Shkolnikov (2019).
Harmonic dynamics of the abelian sandpile.
Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences (online first).
DOI: 10.1073/pnas.1812015116 .

Moritz Lang and Mikhail Shkolnikov (2018).
Harmonic dynamics of the Abelian sandpile.
ArXiv preprint arXiv:1806.10823.

Sandpile Monomorphisms

The sandpile monomorphisms from NxM to s(N+1)-1 x s(M+1)-1 domains are explained in our preprint:

Moritz Lang and Mikhail Shkolnikov (2018).
Sandpile monomorphisms and harmonic functions.
ArXiv preprint arXiv:1904.12209.
