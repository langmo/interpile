Abelian sandpiles and their harmonic dynamics

The identity of the Abelian sandpile group shows smooth 1-periodic dynamics induced by harmonic fields. This website summarizes results on the properties of these dynamics, and also provides an open-source software solution called InterPile to interactively explore and manipulate sandpiles on various domains, as well as to generate movies showing the dynamics of the sandpile identity or any other recurrent configuration induced by arbitrary harmonic functions and for arbitrarily defined domains.

Videos and Software

To access the videos showing the sandpile identity dynamics, visit our Videos section. To create own videos showing the sandpile identity dynamics, download InterPile.
